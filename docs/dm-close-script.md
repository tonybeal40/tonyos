# DM Close Script (Pilot)

## First response after interest
“Awesome. Quick fit check: what’s your current lead flow process, and where do deals usually get stuck?”

## Diagnose questions
1) How are leads captured today?
2) How are they followed up?
3) What conversion bottleneck hurts most right now?
4) Who on your team owns pipeline daily?

## Positioning line
“Based on that, this is exactly what the Tony OS pilot fixes: pipeline visibility, message clarity, and lead prioritization in one workflow.”

## Offer line
“I run this as a 14-day sprint for $2,500. You get implementation + templates + two optimization reviews.”

## Close question
“Do you want to start this week or next week?”

## If hesitant
“Totally fair. Want me to send a one-page implementation plan and you can decide after reviewing?”

## If no response follow-up (24h)
“Still open to seeing the one-page pilot plan? It’ll show exact steps and timeline.”
