    Natoli Monday CRM (Starter) - Step-By-Step

    Keep it simple first. Enforce ownership, tiering, next action, and stage. Everything else is phase 2.

    1. Lock rules: Accounts board is the spine. No orphan contacts. No orphan opportunities.
    2. Build 3 boards: Accounts, Contacts, Opportunities. Do not add extra boards day one.
    3. Require fields: Tier, Fit, Assigned Rep, Stage, Next Action, Due Date.
    4. Automations: Stale flag at 30 days. Require due date when next action set.
    5. Dashboards: Pipeline value, weighted forecast, stage breakdown, rep breakdown, Tier 1 coverage.
    6. Weekly cadence: 15 minutes: update next actions and last touch before forecast review.

    What reps do (30 seconds)

    • Select account
    • Confirm Tier + Fit
    • Set stage and value
    • Choose next action + due date
    • Log last touch type

    What leadership gets (10 seconds)

    • Pipeline and forecast confidence
    • Which reps are moving deals
    • Which accounts are stale
    • Tier 1 coverage
