Operating Model

Natoli Revenue Opportunities System

A disciplined RevOps process designed for credibility, signal quality, and clean routing across Marketing, Sales, and Technical teams

Executive Overview

Simple, defensible sequence you can explain in under a minute

  1. Define the MarketIdentify Targets
  2. Cultivate the DatabaseCapture, Vet, Categorize
  3. Tier PersonasDecision Impact, Risk Ownership
  4. Develop Tailored CampaignsBuild Relationships
  5. Execute CampaignsOutreach
  6. Track EngagementMonitor Signals
  7. Route OpportunitiesMarketing, Sales, Technical
  8. Build the ForecastValidated Signals, Reduced Volatility
  9. Review & Optimize OutreachTargets, Messaging, Opportunities
Operating Principle
RevOps is not activity. RevOps is decision discipline.

Natoli Revenue Opportunities System, Deeper Look

Click each step to see the intent, governance, and why it matters

1 Define the Market
  • Identify target industries, environments, and account types
  • Focus on markets where credibility, risk reduction, and expertise matter
  • Align leadership on what good targets look like
2 Cultivate the Database
  • Capture contacts from approved, trusted sources
  • Vet data for accuracy, relevance, and role legitimacy
  • Categorize by account, persona, and priority level
3 Tier Personas
  • Define decision-makers, technical influencers, and stakeholders
  • Tier personas by decision impact and risk responsibility
  • Eliminate focus on roles that cannot drive outcomes
4 Develop Tailored Campaigns
  • Build campaigns around persona needs and operating risk
  • Deliver technical credibility, not sales pressure
  • Focus on relationship-building over immediate conversion
5 Execute Campaigns
  • Run disciplined, coordinated outreach across channels
  • Follow approved messaging and cadence standards
  • Ensure consistency regardless of team or touchpoint
6 Track Engagement
  • Monitor engagement quality, not just activity volume
  • Identify intent, urgency, and readiness signals
  • Separate real interest from passive consumption
7 Route Opportunities
  • Route signals to the right team: Marketing, Sales, or Technical
  • Apply clear qualification rules before pipeline entry
  • Maintain ownership and accountability at every stage
8 Build the Forecast
  • Base forecasts on validated engagement and decision signals
  • Surface risk early and transparently
  • Reduce volatility caused by optimism or assumptions
9 Review & Optimize Outreach
  • Review performance by target, persona, and campaign
  • Refine messaging, targeting, and cadence based on signal
  • Identify new campaign opportunities and scale what works
Wash, rinse, and repeat this process to build brand awareness, followers, and loyalists.
