📊 Move Score Explained

Move Score blends financial impact, urgency, and confidence into a single prioritization signal (0-100). Higher = move first.

⚠️ Urgency Levels

URGENT: Over 180 days idle, needs immediate action
HIGH: 90-180 days, should be addressed soon
MEDIUM: Under 90 days, monitor regularly

❓ How to Use This Command Center

📊 Step 1: Choose Your Source

Click ACC, HAGLIN, or Combined at the top to switch inventory views. All data updates automatically.

📋 Step 2: Explore the Tabs

  • Dashboard - KPIs & quick actions
  • Inventory - Full searchable list
  • Rankings - Top 25 products
  • Export - Download & deploy

🔍 Step 3: Search & Filter

In Full Inventory, use the search box and dropdowns to find specific items by SKU, filter by source, or sort by value/score.

📥 Step 4: Export Your Data

  • Export CSV - Spreadsheet download
