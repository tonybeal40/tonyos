2026 Budget Allocation Summary

  • Tier 1 (APR, Pharmaceutical Online, Contract Pharma): Carry the majority of commercial spend. These are buyer-dense channels.
  • Tier 2 (IPT, EPM, Manufacturing Chemist, EPR, Tablets and Capsules): Selective, targeted campaigns for specific audiences or scientific credibility.
