5 Our Communication Approach

All outreach follows a problem-first approach. We identify challenges relevant to your operation and offer to help — no aggressive pitches, no empty follow-ups.

Initial Introduction

First contact that identifies industry-specific pain points and offers a conversation about root-cause solutions.

Follow-Up

Brief, respectful check-in acknowledging timing constraints while leaving the door open for future needs.

Technical Problem

Response to a specific issue mentioned — sticking, capping, weight variation — with offer to troubleshoot.

Trade Show Lead

Post-event follow-up continuing a conversation started at INTERPHEX, AAPS, or similar industry events.

Referral Introduction

Connection made through a mutual contact or colleague with context on why they suggested reaching out.

Reconnection

Re-engagement with previous customers or contacts, acknowledging the gap and offering current support.
