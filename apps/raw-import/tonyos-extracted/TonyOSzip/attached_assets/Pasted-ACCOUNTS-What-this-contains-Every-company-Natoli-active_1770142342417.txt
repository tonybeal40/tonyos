ACCOUNTS

What this contains:
Every company Natoli actively tracks or could sell to.

Examples:

Pfizer

Catalent Pharma Solutions

Perrigo

NutraScience Labs

Lonza

Contract Pharmacal Corp

Local compounding pharmacies

Veterinary tablet manufacturers

Rules:

One row per company

Always has an owner

Always has a status (New / Active / Lapsed)

Always has a next action if Active

Think:

“Who are we selling to or could sell to”

CONTACTS

What this contains:
People inside those accounts.

Examples:

Jane Smith – Director of Manufacturing – Pfizer

Mark Lopez – Process Engineer – Catalent

Sarah Chen – Quality Manager – Lonza

Mike Davis – Operations Lead – NutraScience

Rules:

Must be linked to an Account

Can have multiple contacts per account

Can mark one as Primary

Think:

“Who do we actually talk to”

ACTIVITY

What this contains:
Every meaningful interaction with a human.

Examples:

Call with Ops Director (discussed tooling wear)

Email reply about compression force limits

Teams meeting recap

Follow-up after site visit

LinkedIn message with real response

Not included:

Email opens

Automated sends

Guesswork

Think:

“Proof we’re moving the relationship forward”

PIPELINE

What this contains:
Potential or active revenue opportunities.

Examples:

“Tooling replacement for NP500 press – Q2”

“New D tooling for nutraceutical line”

“CT scan services for formulation team”

“Engineering training package – March”

Rules:

Tied to an Account

Has a stage

Has a rough value or impact

Think:

“Where money is being discussed”

NEW ACCOUNT INTAKE

What this contains:
Accounts not yet qualified.

Examples:

CSV upload from Sales Navigator

Conference attendee list

Purchased industry list

Inbound website form submissions

State:

Status = New

Needs research = Yes

No outreach yet

Think:

“Potential accounts that need review before engagement”

LAPSED ACCOUNTS

What this contains:
Previously active customers with no activity in 12+ months.

Examples:

Former tooling customer inactive since 2022

Past training attendee with no follow-up

Old distributor account

Rules:

Must have historical activity

Requires re-engagement strategy

Think:

“Dormant value we can wake up”

ASSETS & DOCS

What this contains:
Anything that can be sent externally or referenced internally.

Examples:

Tooling brochures

Tablet defect charts

Validation documents

CT scan service overview

Training decks

Approved sales PDFs

Rules:

Versioned

Approved

No personal files

Think:

“What we’re allowed to send”

UPLOAD CSV

What this contains:
A controlled entry point for new data.

Examples:

Sales Nav export

Apollo export

Event list

Spreadsheet from a rep

What happens:

User uploads file

Maps columns

System creates New Account records

Writes to Monday

Think:

“How data enters safely”
