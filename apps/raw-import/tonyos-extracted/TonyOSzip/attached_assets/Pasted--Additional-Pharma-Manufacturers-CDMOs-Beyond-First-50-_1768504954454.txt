🧠 Notes on the List
Why these matter

These companies are in one or more of these buckets:

Oral solid dose drug product manufacturing

Pharmaceutical APIs (including steroids / hormone precursors)

Potent compound / HPAPI manufacturing

CDMO services with hormone-adjacent capability

Multi-client production with segregated suite infrastructure

What this isn’t

This list doesn’t include:

Clinics

Telehealth hormone providers

Wellness / anti-aging practices

Compounding pharmacies

Supplement / nutraceutical brands

That keeps the signal strong.
