Persona 1
Director of Engineering (Solid Dose)
Reliability & Uptime

Job to be done: Guarantee predictable tablet production with maximum uptime and tool life.

Top pains
  • Unplanned downtime from tool or press issues.
  • Inconsistent tablet quality causing batch losses and rework.
  • Vendors who can’t explain root causes or give clear fixes.
What they want
  • Reliable presses and tooling that “just run.”
  • Root-cause clarity when something goes wrong.
  • Confidence that future batches will behave the same way.
Natoli Engineering fit
  • Tooling design and press troubleshooting.
  • Programs to extend tool life and reduce downtime.
Natoli Scientific fit
  • Compaction analytics (USP <1062>).
  • CT-scan and data to support engineering decisions.
Messaging angle
Use: “More uptime, fewer surprises, clear root causes.”
Persona 2
Director of R&D / Formulation
Data & Scale-Up

Job to be done: Turn lab formulations into robust commercial products without nasty surprises.

Top pains
  • Scale-up failures that delay launches.
  • Poor or incomplete compaction data.
  • Powder behavior changing between R&D, pilot, and production.
What they want
  • Clean, interpretable compaction profiles.
  • Predictable scale-up with fewer iterations.
  • Data that supports regulatory confidence.
Natoli Scientific fit
  • Analytical compaction studies and predictive models.
  • CT-scan work to understand internal tablet structure.
Natoli Engineering fit
  • Tooling and press recommendations that match R&D data.
  • Bridging R&D presses to production equipment.
Messaging angle
Use: “Data-backed scale-up from formulation bench to commercial line.”
Persona 3
Process / Compaction Engineer
Speed & Diagnostics

Job to be done: Fix compaction problems fast and keep them from coming back.

Top pains
  • Repeated defects (capping, lamination, hardness issues).
  • No clear root cause between tooling, powder, and press.
  • Pressure from leadership to “just fix it” under time pressure.
What they want
  • Fast, practical troubleshooting support.
  • Data and visuals that explain what’s happening.
  • Adjustments they can trust on the next run.
Natoli Engineering fit
  • Press setup and parameter guidance.
  • Tooling inspection and upgrade paths.
Natoli Scientific fit
  • Compaction curves and failure analysis.
  • CT-scan insight into defect mechanisms.
Messaging angle
Use: “Quick clarity on why tablets are failing and what to change next.”
